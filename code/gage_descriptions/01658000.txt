This river segment follows part of the flow of Mattawoman Creek near Pomonkey, MD. Gage 01658000 is located in Charles County, MD (Lat 38 35'46.1", Long 77 03'21.7") approximately 1.2 miles southeast of Pomonkey. Drainage area is 54.8 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1949 and has been taking data periodically until now. The low flow is affected by ground-water diversions from municipal well fields at Waldorf and St. Charles. There is also a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
