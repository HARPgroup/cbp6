This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rappahannock River near Fredericksburg, VA. Gage 01668000 is located in Spotsylvania County, VA (Lat 38 18'30", Long 77 31'46") approximately 5.3 miles upstream from Fredericksburg, VA. Drainage area is 1,595 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1907 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
