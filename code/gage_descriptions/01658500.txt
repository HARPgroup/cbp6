This river segment follows part of the flow of South Fork Quantico Creek near Independent Hill, VA. Gage 01658500 is located in Prince William County, VA (Lat 38 35'14", Long 77 25'44") approximately 3.4 miles south of Independent Hill. Drainage area is 7.62 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1951 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
