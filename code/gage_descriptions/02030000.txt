This river segment follows part of the flow of the Hardware River below Briery Creek, near Scottsville, VA. Gage 02030000 is located in Fluvanna County, VA (Lat 37 48'45", Long 78 27'20") approximately 2.4 miles northeast of Scottsville. Drainage area is 116 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and has been taking data periodically until now. It was published as "below Briery Run" prior to October 1990. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
