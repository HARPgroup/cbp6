This river segment follows part of the flow of the St. Mary's River at Great Mills, MD. Gage 01661500 is located in St. Mary's County, MD (Lat 38 14'30.3", Long 76 30'13.2") approximately 0.3 miles downstream from Western Branch. Drainage area is 24.0 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1946 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is occasional regulation by the reservoir on Western Branch of St. Marys River, 2.0 mi upstream since 1975, total capacity 3,200 acre feet. There is also a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
