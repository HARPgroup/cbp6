This river segment follows part of the flow of Cedar Run near Catlett, VA. Gage 01656000 is located in Faquier County, VA (Lat 38 38'12", Long 77 37'31") approximately 1.4 miles southeast of Catlett. Drainage area is 93.4 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1950 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is some diurnal fluctuation at low flow from an unknown cause.
