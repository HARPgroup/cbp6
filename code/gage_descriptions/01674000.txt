This river segment follows part of the flow of the Mattaponi River near Bowling Green, VA. Gage 01674000 is located in Caroline County, VA (Lat 38 03'42", Long 77 23'10") approximately 2.2 miles northeast of Bowling Green, VA. Drainage area is 256 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and is still taking data currently. Some diurnal fluctuation from gristmill upstream on Po River.
