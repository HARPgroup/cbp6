This river segment follows part of the flow of Gunpowder Falls at Glencoe, MD. Gage 01582500 is located in Baltimore County, MD (Lat 39 32'58.9", Long 76 38'10") approximately 0.7 miles upstream from Piney Creek. Drainage area is 160 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1977 and has been taking data periodically until now. Flow regulated by Prettyboy Reservoir, 12 mi upstream, beginning April 10, 1933, for water supply of Baltimore City (usable capacity, 20,000,000,000 gal; dead storage, 1,080,000,000 gal). U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
