This river segment follows part of the flow of Rock Creek at Sherrill Drive, Washington DC. Gage 01648000 is located at (Lat 38 58'21.0", Long 77 02'24.0"). Drainage area is 62.2 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1929 and has been taking data continuously until now. Flow is affected by two upstream reservoirs which control flow from about 25 sq. miles, Needwood Lake on Rock Creek since Sept. 1966 and Bernard Frank Lake on North Branch Rock Creek since February 1968. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
