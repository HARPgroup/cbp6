This river segment follows part of the flow of Antietam Creek near Sharpsburg, MD. Gage 01619500 is located in Washington County, MD (Lat 39 26'59.2", Long 77 43'48.7") approximately 1.0 miles southeast of Sharpsburg, MD. Drainage area is 281 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1897 and is still taking data currently. Some diurnal fluctuation caused by power plant upstream from station. Since 1928, records include pumpage from the Potomac River for municipal supply of Hagerstown. This water later enters Antietam Creek upstream from station as sewage. Records adjusted for monthly and annual mean inflow from January 1930 to September 2011. U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
