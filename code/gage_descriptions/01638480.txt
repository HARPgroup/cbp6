This river segment follows part of the flow of Catoctin Creek near Taylorstown, VA. Gage 01638480 is located in Loudoun County, VA (Lat 39 15'18", Long 77 34'36") approximately 3.2 miles downstream from Milltown Creek. Drainage area is 89.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1971 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
