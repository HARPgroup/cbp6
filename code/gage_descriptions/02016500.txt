This river segment follows part of the flow of the James River near Lick Run, VA. Gage 02016500 is located in Botetourt County, VA (Lat 37 46'25", Long 79 47'05") approximately 1.8 miles south of Iron Gate, VA. Drainage area is 1,371 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data currently. Flow has been regulated since December 1979 by Lake Moomaw (station 02011795) 43.7 mi upstream from station; since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 71.7 mi upstream; and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 74.8 mi upstream, amount unknown.
