This river segment follows part of the flow of the Appomattox River at Mattoax, VA. Gage 02040000 is located in Amelia County, VA (Lat 37 25'17", Long 77 51'33") approximately 3.7 miles upstream from Flat Creek. Drainage area is 725 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1900 and is still taking data currently. There is a National Weather Service gage-height telemeter at station.
