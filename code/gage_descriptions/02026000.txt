This river segment follows part of the flow of the James River at Bent Creek, VA. Gage 02026000 is located in Nelson County, VA (Lat 37 32'10", Long 78 49'47") approximately 1.3 miles southeast of Gladstone, VA. Drainage area is 3,649 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data currently. Large diurnal fluctuation is caused by powerplants upstream from station. Flow has been regulated since December 1979 by Lake Moomaw (station 02011795) 158.3 mi upstream; since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 186.3 mi upstream; and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 189.4 mi upstream, amount unknown.
