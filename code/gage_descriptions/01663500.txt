This river segment follows part of the flow of the Hazel River at Rixeyville, VA. Gage 01663500 is located in Culpeper County, VA (Lat 38 35'30", Long 77 57'55") approximately 1.2 miles northeast of Rixeyville, VA. Drainage area is 285 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
