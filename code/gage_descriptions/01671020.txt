This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Anna River at Hart Corner near Doswell, VA. Gage 01671020 is located in Hanover County, VA (Lat 37 51'00", Long 77 25'41") approximately 2.1 miles east of Doswell, VA. Drainage area is 462 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1979 and has been taking data periodically until now. Diversion at a point 0.8 mi upstream from station since 1973. Maximum discharge, 12,000 ft³/s, from rating curve extended above 10,100 ft³/s.
