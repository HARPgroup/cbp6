This river segment follows part of the flow of Cowpasture River near Clifton Forge, VA. Gage 02016000 is located in Alleghany County, VA (Lat 37 47'30", Long 79 45'35") approximately 4.0 miles southeast of Clifton Forge. Drainage area is 461 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and has been taking data periodically until now. Low flows affected by springs and by occasional regulation from unknown source.
