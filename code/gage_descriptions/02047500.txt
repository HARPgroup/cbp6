This river segment follows part of the flow of the Blackwater River, a tributary of the Meherrin River. Gage 02047500 is located in Surry County, VA (Lat 37°01'30", Long 76°52'30") approximately 32 miles southeast of Petersburg, VA. Drainage area is 290 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1941 and is still taking data but there is a gap from 1987-01-09 to 1988-07-27. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
