This river segment follows part of the flow of the Mattaponi River near Beulahville, VA. Gage 01674500 is located in King William County, VA (Lat 37 53'02", Long 77 09'55") approximately 2.5 miles northeast of Beulahville, VA. Drainage area is 603 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1941 and has been taking data periodically until now. Diurnal fluctuation at times during low flow caused by gristmill on Po River. Maximum discharge, 16,900 cfs, from rating curve extended above 11,760 cfs.
