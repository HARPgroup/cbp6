This river segment follows part of the flow of Big Elk Creek near Elk Mills, MD. Gage 01495000 is located in Cecil County, MD (Lat 39 40'03.3", Long 75 49'32.1") approximately 4.3 miles north of Elkton. Drainage area is 51.6 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1932 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
