This river segment follows part of the flow of the James River at Scottsville, VA. Gage 02029000 is located in Albemarle County, VA (Lat 37 47'50", Long 78 29'30") approximately 6.8 miles upstream from Hardware River. Drainage area is 4,581 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1924 and is still taking data currently. Large diurnal fluctuation is caused by powerplants upstream from station. Flow has been regulated since December 1979 by Lake Moomaw (station 02011795) 197.5 mi upstream; since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 225.5 mi upstream; and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 228.6 mi upstream, amount unknown.
