This river segment follows part of the flow of the Choptank River near Greensboro, MD. Gage 01491000 is located in Caroline County, MD (Lat 38 59'49.9", Long 75 47'08.9") approximately 2.0 miles northeast of Greensboro. Drainage area is 113 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1948 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are diversions for irrigation of about 500 acres, upstream from station and a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
