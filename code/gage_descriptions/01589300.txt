This river segment follows part of the flow of Gwynns Falls at Villa Nova, MD. Gage 01589300 is located in Baltimore County, VA (Lat 39 20'45.2", Long 76 43'59.5") approximately 1.1 miles west of Baltimore County limits. Drainage area is 32.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1957 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is slight diurnal fluctuation at times from an unknown source upstream from the station. There is also a small diversion for irrigation upstream from the station and a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at the station.
