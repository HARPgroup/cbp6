This river segment follows part of the flow of Clement Creek near Clements, MD. Gage 01661050 is located in St. Mary's County, MD (Lat 38 19'59.9", Long 76 43'30.0") approximately 5.7 miles northwest of Leonardtown. Drainage area is 18.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1968 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is occasional regulation from an unknown source upstream from station and U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
