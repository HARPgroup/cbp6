This river segment follows part of the flow of Cameron Run at Alexandria, VA. Gage 01653000 is located in Alexandria City, VA (Lat 38 48'23", Long 77 06'36") approximately 0.5 miles east of the U.S. Army Quartermaster Depot. Drainage area is 33.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1955 and has been taking data periodically until now. Some regulation by Lake Barcroft, formerly Alexandria Reservoir, on Holmes Run 3.6 mi upstream, usable capacity 2,092 acre-ft. Maximum discharge, 19,900 ft³/s, from rating curve extended above 2,500 ft³/s on basis of culvert computations of peak flow for main channel and bypass channels. Water-quality records for some periods have been collected at this location.
