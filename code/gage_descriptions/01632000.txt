This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Fork Shenandoah River at Cootes Store, VA. Gage 01632000 is located in Rockingham County, VA (Lat 38 38'13", Long 78 51'11") approximately 3.7 miles upstream from Linville Creek. Drainage area is 210 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and has been taking data regularly until now. There is a National Weather Service gage-height telemeter and a Virginia Department of Emergency Services gage-height radio transmitter at the station.
