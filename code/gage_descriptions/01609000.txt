This river segment follows part of the flow of Town Creek near Oldtown, MD. Gage 01609000 is located in Allegany County, MD (Lat 39 33'11.6", Long 78 33'18.0") approximately 3.0 miles northeast of Oldtown, MD. Drainage area is 148 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at the station.
