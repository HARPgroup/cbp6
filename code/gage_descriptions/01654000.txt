This river segment follows part of the flow of Accotink Creek near Annandale, VA. Gage 01654000 is located in Fairfax County, VA (Lat 38 48'46", Long 77 13'43") approximately 2.3 miles southwest of Annandale. Drainage area is 23.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1947 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
