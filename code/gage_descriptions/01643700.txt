This river segment follows part of the flow of the Goose Creek near Middleburg, VA. Gage 01643700 is located in Loudon County, VA (Lat 38 59'11", Long 77 47'49") approximately 3.4 miles northwest of Middleburg. Drainage area is 122 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1965 and has been taking data for most of the time until present, except for a period between Sept. 1996 and June 2001.
