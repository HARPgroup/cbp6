This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rappahannock River at Remington, VA. Gage 01664000 is located in Faquier County, VA (Lat 38 31'50", Long 77 48'50") approximately 2.5 miles downstream from Hazel River. Drainage area is 619 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and has been taking data periodically until now. National Weather Service gage-height telemeter at station. Maximum discharge, 90,000 cfs, from rating curve extended above 43,000 cfs on basis of slope-area measurement of peak flow.
