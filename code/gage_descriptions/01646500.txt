This river segment follows part of the flow of the Potomac River near Washington, DC. The gage is located in Montgomery County, MD (Lat 38 56'59.2", Long 77 07'39.5) approximately 1.8 miles east of Langley, Fairfax County. Drainage area is 11,560 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1930 and is still taking data currently. Low flow is affected slightly prior to July 1981 by the Stony River Reservoir, since December 1950, by the Savage River Reservoir (see station 01597500), and since July 1981, by the Jennings Randolph Lake. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
