This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Fork of the Shenandoah River at Front Royal, VA. Gage 01631000 is located in Warren County, VA (Lat 38 54'50", Long 78 12'40") approximately 1.0 miles west of Front Royal, VA. Drainage area is 1,634 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1899 and is still taking data currently. Large diurnal fluctuation at low and medium flow caused by powerplants upstream from station prior to 1954; occasional large diurnal fluctuation thereafter. National Weather Service gage-height telemeter at station. Maximum discharge, 130,000 cfs, from rating curve extended above 92,000 cfs on basis of slope-area measurement of peak flow.
