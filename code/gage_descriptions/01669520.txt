This river segment follows part of the flow of Dragon Swamp near Mascot, VA. Gage 01669520 is located in King and Queen County, VA (Lat 37 38'01", Long 76 41'48") approximately 3.3 miles west of Warner, VA. Drainage area is 109 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1981 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
