This river segment follows part of the flow of the Chickahominy River near Providence Forge, VA. Gage 02042500 is located in New Kent County, VA (Lat 37 26'10", Long 77 03'40") approximately 1.1 miles southwest from Providence Forge. Drainage area is 251 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
