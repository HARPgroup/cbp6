This river segment follows part of the flow of the Conococheague Creek at Fairview, MD. Gage 01614500 is located in Washington County, MD (Lat 39 42'59", Long 77 49'29.2") approximately 6.5 miles nothwest of Hagerstown, MD. Drainage area is 494 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and has been taking data periodically until now. Diversions for irrigation upstream from station. U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
