This river segment follows part of the flow of Opequon Creek near Martinsburg, WV. Gage 01616500 is located in Berkeley County, WV (Lat 39 25'25", Long 77 56'20") approximately 3.0 miles southeast of Martinsburg, WV. Drainage area is 273 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1905 and is still taking data currently. Maximum discharge, 23,400 cfs, from rating curve extended above 7,100 cfs. Some diurnal fluctuation at low flow caused by upstream mills in Virginia and since July 18, 1988, by wastewater treatment plant, 1,000 ft upstream from Opequon Creek near Berryville, VA (01615000); drainage area 57.4 mi².
