This river segment follows part of the flow of Sideling Hill Creek near Bellegrove, MD. Gage 01610155 is located in Washington County, MD (Lat 39 38'58.3", Long 78 20'38.9") approximately 4.0 miles south of Bellegrove. Drainage area is 102 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1967 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
