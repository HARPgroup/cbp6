This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Fork of the Shenandoah River near Luray, VA. Gage 01629500 is located in Page County, VA (Lat 38 38'46", Long 78 32'06") approximately 4.1 miles west of Luray, VA. Drainage area is 1,372 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data currently. Diurnal fluctuation at low and medium flow caused by powerplant 10 mi upstream from station. Virginia Department of Emergency Services and National Weather Service gage-height transmitters at station. Maximum discharge, 112,000 cfs, from rating curve extended above 86,300 cfs.
