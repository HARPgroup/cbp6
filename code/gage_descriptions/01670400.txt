This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Anna River near Partlow, VA. Gage 01670400 is located in Louisa County, VA (Lat 38 00'46", Long 77 42'05") approximately 3.8 miles southwest of Partlow, VA. Drainage area is 342 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1978 and is still taking data currently. Flow regulated by Lake Anna, capacity, 373,000 acre-ft, 0.5 mi upstream. Maximum discharge, 11,700 cfs, from rating curve extended above 7,200 cfs. Water-quality records for some prior periods have been collected at this location by the Virginia Department of Water Quality - Water Division.
