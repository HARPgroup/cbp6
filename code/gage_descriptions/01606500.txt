This river segment follows part of the flow of the Potomac River at Barnum, WV. Gage 01595800 is located in Mineral County, VA (Lat 39 26'42.4", Long 79 06'38.9") approximately 4.0 miles southwest of Piedmont W. VA. Drainage area is 266 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1966 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this are a that would affect the flow conditions.
