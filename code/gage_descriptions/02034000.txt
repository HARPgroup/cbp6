This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rivanna River at Palmyra, VA. Gage 02034000 is located in Fluvanna County, VA (Lat 37 51'28", Long 78 15'58") approximately 0.5 miles upstream from Cunningham Creek. Drainage area is 663 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1933 and is still taking data currently. Some diurnal fluctuation at times mostly at low and medium flow is caused by South Fork Rivanna River Reservoir, and diversions and discharge upstream at Charlottesville.
