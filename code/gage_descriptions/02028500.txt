This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rockfish River, near Greenfield, VA. Gage 02028500 is located in Nelson County, VA (Lat 37 52'10", Long 78 49'25") approximately 4.1 miles south of Greenfield. Drainage area is 94.8 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
