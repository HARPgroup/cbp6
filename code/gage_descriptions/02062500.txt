This river segment follows part of the flow of the Roanoke River. Gage 02062500 is located in Campbell County, VA (Lat 37°02'22.0", Long 78°56'44.6") approximately 28 miles southeast of Lynchburg, VA. Drainage area is 2404 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1923 and is still taking data. The Smith Mountain Lake and Leesville Dams are located in this area and may affect the flow.
