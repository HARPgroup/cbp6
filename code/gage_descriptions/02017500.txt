This river segment follows part of the flow of Johns Creek at New Castle, VA. Gage 02017500 is located in Craig County, VA (Lat 37 30'22", Long 80 06'25"). Drainage area is 105 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 2006 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
