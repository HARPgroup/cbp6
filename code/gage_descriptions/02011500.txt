This river segment follows part of the flow of Back Creek near Mountain Creek, VA. Gage 02011500 is located in Bath County, VA (Lat 38 04'10", Long 79 53'50") approximately 2.1 miles south from Mountain Grove, VA. Drainage area is 134 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1951 and is still taking data currently. Flow regulated since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 11.3 mi upstream, amount unknown, and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 14.4 mi upstream, amount unknown.
