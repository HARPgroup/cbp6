This river segment follows part of the flow of Aquia Creek near Garrisonville, VA. Gage 01660400 is located in Stafford County, VA (Lat 38 29'25", Long 77 26'02") approximately 1.1 miles northwest of Garrisonville. Drainage area is 35 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1971 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
