This river segment follows part of the flow of the Calfpasture River above Mill Creek at Goshen, VA. Gage 02020500 is located in Rockbridge County, VA (Lat 37 59'16", Long 79 29'38"). Drainage area is 141 sq. miles and is 400 ft upstream from Mill Creek. This gage started taking data in 1938 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is a Virginia Department of Emergency Services gage-height radio transmitter at the station.
