This river segment follows part of the flow of the Savage River near Barton, MD. Gage 01596500 is located in Garrett County, MD (Lat 39 34'12.2", Long 79 06'07.0") approximately 5.4 miles northwest of Barton. Drainage area is 49.1 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1948 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is a U.S. Army Corps of Engineers satellite data-collection platform at the station.
