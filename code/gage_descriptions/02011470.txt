This river segment follows part of the flow of Back Creek at Sunrise, VA. Gage 02011470 is located in Bath County, VA (Lat 38 11'25", Long 79 48'43") approximately 7.6 miles northeast of Mountain Grove, VA. Drainage area is 75.6 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1984 and is still taking data currently. Flow regulated since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 0.5 mi upstream, amount unknown. Virginia Power gage-height transmitter at station, receiver at Back Creek Dam. U.S. Army Corps of Engineers satellite precipitation and gage-height telemeter at station.
