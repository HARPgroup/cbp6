This river segment follows part of the flow of North Anna River near Doswell, VA. Gage 01671025 is located in Hanover County, VA (Lat 37 49'32", Long 77 25'35") approximately 3.0 miles southwest of Doswell, VA. Drainage area is 467 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 2004 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
