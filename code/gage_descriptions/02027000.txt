This river segment follows part of the flow of the Tye River, near Lovingston, VA. Gage 02027000 is located in Nelson County, VA (Lat 37 42'55", Long 78 58'55") approximately 6.8 miles southwest of Lovingston. Drainage area is 93 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
