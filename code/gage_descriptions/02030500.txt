This river segment follows part of the flow of the Slate River, near Arvonia, VA. Gage 02030500 is located in Buckingham County, VA (Lat 37 42'10", Long 78 22'40") approximately 1.8 miles northwest of Arvonia. Drainage area is 226 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1926 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
