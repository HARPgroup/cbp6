This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Fork of the Shenandoah River near Strasburg, VA. Gage 01634000 is located in Warren County, VA (Lat 38 58'36", Long 78 20'11") approximately 1.5 miles southeast of Strasburg, VA. Drainage area is 770 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data currently. Large diurnal fluctuation at low and medium flow from unknown cause. Water-level elevations at the site were affected during the 1992-93 water years by construction of a new bridge about 50 ft downstream from the gage. National Weather Service gage-height telemeter at station. Maximum discharge, 114,000 cfs, from rating curve extended above 46,000 cfs.
