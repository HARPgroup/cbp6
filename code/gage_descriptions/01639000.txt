This river segment follows part of the flow of the Monocacy River near Bridgeport, MD. Gage 01639000 is located in Frederick County, MD (Lat 39 40'44.6", Long 77 14'04.3") approximately 0.9 miles upstream of Cattail Branch. Drainage area is 173 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is occasional regulation at low flow from Lake Heritage and other unknown sources upstream from station. There is also a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at the station.
