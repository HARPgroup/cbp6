This river segment follows part of the flow of the Maury River at Rockbridge Baths, VA. Gage 02021500 is located in Rockbridge County, VA (Lat 37 54'26", Long 79 25'20") approximately 1.0 miles upstream from Hays Creek. Drainage area is 329 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and is still taking data currently. Since 1966, some regulation has taken place at times by Lake Merriweather on Little Calfpasture River.
