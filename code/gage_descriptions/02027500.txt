This river segment follows part of the flow of the Piney River, at Piney River, VA. Gage 02027500 is located in Nelson County, VA (Lat 37 42'08", Long 79 01'40") approximately 0.2 miles southwest of Piney River Post Office. Drainage area is 47.7 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1949 and has been taking data continuously until now. Periodic dewatering of upstream quarries adds a small amount of inflow at times.
