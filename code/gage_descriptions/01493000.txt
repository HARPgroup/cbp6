This river segment follows part of the flow of Unicorn Branch near Millington, MD. Gage 01493000 is located in Queen Anne's County, MD (Lat 39 14'59.8", long 75 51'41.4") approximately 1.4 miles southwest of Millington. Drainage area is 19.7 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1948 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is occasional regulation at low and medium flow by Unicorn Lake Dam upstream from the gage station and a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
