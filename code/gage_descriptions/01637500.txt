This river segment follows part of the flow of Catoctin Creek near Middletown, MD. Gage 01637500 is located in Frederick County, MD (Lat 39 25'38.1", Long 77 33'22.2") approximately 1.3 miles south of Middletown. Drainage area is 66.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1947 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
