This river segment follows part of the flow of the Little River near Doswell, VA. Gage 01671100 is located in Hanover County, VA (Lat 37 52'21", Long 77 30'48") approximately 0.8 miles southwest of Verdon. Drainage area is 107 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1961 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
