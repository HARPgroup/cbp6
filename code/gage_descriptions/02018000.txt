This river segment follows part of the flow of Craig Creek at Parr, VA. Gage 02018000 is located in Botetourt County, VA (Lat 37 39'57", long 79 54'42") approximately 0.2 miles northeast of Horton. Drainage area is 329 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1925 and is still taking data currently. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
