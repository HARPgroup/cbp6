This river segment follows part of the flow of the Monocacy River near Frederick, MD. Gage 01643000 is located in Frederick County, MD (Lat 39 24'10.2", Long 77 21'57.9") approximately 2.0 miles east of Frederick, MD. Drainage area is 817 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1929 and has been taking data periodically until now. Occasional regulation at low and medium flows since September 1972 by Linganore Reservoir, total capacity, 883,200,000 gal, 2.8 mi upstream from station. U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
