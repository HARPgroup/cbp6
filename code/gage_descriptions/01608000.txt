This river segment follows part of the flow of the Southern Fork of the South Branch Potomac River near Moorefield, WV. Gage 01608000 is located in Hardy County, WV (Lat 39 00'44", Long 78 57'23") approximately 3.5 miles south of Moorefield, WV. Drainage area is 277 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and has been taking data periodically until now. Maximum discharge, 110,000 cfs, from rating curve extended above 39,000 cfs on basis of slope-area measurement of peak flow, highest since 1878. Beginning in 1973, the flow from 92.7 mi² upstream from station has been partially controlled, but not diverted, by several floodwater-detention reservoirs with a total combined detention capacity of 19,870 acre-ft.
