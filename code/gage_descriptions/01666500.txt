This river segment follows part of the flow of the Robinson River near Locust Dale, VA. Gage 01666500 is located in Madison County, VA (Lat 38 19'30", Long 78 05'45") approximately 2.0 miles southeast of Locust Dale. Drainage area is 179 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and has been taking data periodically until now. There was a flood on October 15, 1942 that reached a stage of 23.9 ft, from floodmarks, discharge, about 44,000 cfs.
