This river segment follows part of the flow of the Mechums River near White Hall, VA. Gage 02031000 is located in Albemarle County, MD (Lat 38 06'09", Long 78 35'35") approximately 4.0 miles southeast of White Hall. Drainage area is 95.3 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and has been taking data periodically until now. Prior to September 1951, this gage was published as Mechum River near Ivy. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
