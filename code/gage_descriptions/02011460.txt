This river segment follows part of the flow of Back Creek near Sunrise, VA. Gage 02011460 is located in Bath County, VA (Lat 38 14'43", Long 79 46'08") approximately 4.8 miles northeast of Sunrise, VA. Drainage area is 60.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1974 and is still taking data currently. Virginia Power gage-height transmitter at station, receiver at Back Creek Dam. Maximum discharge, 17,500 ft³/s, from rating curve extended above 3,800 ft³/s. Water-quality records for some prior periods have been collected at this location.
