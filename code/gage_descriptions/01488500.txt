This river segment follows part of the flow of Marshyhope Creek near Adamsville, DE. Gage 01488500 is located in Kent County, DE (Lat 38 50'58.9", Long 75 40'23.2") approximately 5.5 miles northwest of Snow Hill. Drainage area is 46.8 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is occasional regulation at low and medium flows for irrigation upstream from station and a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
