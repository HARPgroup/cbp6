This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Anna River near Ashland, VA. Gage 01672500 is located in Hanover County, VA (Lat 37 47'48", Long 77 32'57") approximately 4.5 miles northwest of Ashland, VA. Drainage area is 395 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1930 and is still taking data currently. Since 1966, diversion 150 ft upstream from station has averaged less than 0.6 ft³/s. Capacity of diversion is about 1.5 ft³/s. Maximum discharge, 17,100 ft³/s, from rating curve extended above 12,600 ft³/s. Water-quality records for some prior periods have been collected at this location.
