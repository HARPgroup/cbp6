This river segment follows part of the flow of Totopotomoy Creek near Studley, VA. Gage 01673550 is located in Hanover County, VA (Lat 37 39'45", Long 77 15'29") approximately 2.0 miles southeast of Studley, VA. Drainage area is 25.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1977 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
