This river segment follows part of the flow of the James River at Cartersville, VA. Gage 02035000 is located in Goochland County, VA (Lat 37 40'15", Long 78 05'10") approximately 1.8 miles downstream from Willis River. Drainage area is 6,252 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1898 and is still taking data currently. Moderate diurnal fluctuation is caused by powerplants upstream from station. Since 1982, low flows during summer months are augmented by releases from Lake Moomaw, station 02011795.
