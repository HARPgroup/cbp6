This river segment follows part of the flow of the Shenandoah River at Millville, WV. Gage 01636500 is located in Jefferson County, WV (Lat 39 16'55", Long 77 47'22") approximately 1.0 miles upstream from Millville, WV. Drainage area is 3,041 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1895 and is still taking data currently. Some regulation by upstream hydroelectric plants, including that of Potomac Light and Power Company, 0.5 mi upstream from station.
