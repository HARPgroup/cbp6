This river segment follows part of the flow of the Appomattox River near Farmville, VA. Gage 02039500 is located in Cumberland County, MD (Lat 37 18'25", Long 78 23'20") approximately 1.1 miles downstream from Buffalo Creek. Drainage area is 302 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 2006 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is diurnal fluctuation at low flow caused by the Prince Edward Mill 0.2 mi upstream.
