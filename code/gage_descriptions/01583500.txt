This river segment follows part of the flow of Western Run at Western Run, MD. Gage 01583500 is located in Baltimore County, MD (Lat 39 30'38.8", Long 76 40'35.4") approximately 0.3 miles southeast of Western Run, MD. Drainage area is 59.8 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1944 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
