This river segment follows part of the flow of the Middle River near Grottoes, VA. Gage 01625000 is located in Augusta County, VA (Lat 38 15'42", Long 78 51'44") approximately 2.0 miles west of Grottoes. Drainage area is 373 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1927 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are discharges of about 6.0 cfs from wastewater treatment plants upstream from the station. Most of water discharged from treatment plants was diverted from another drainage basin for industrial and municipal supply. There is small diurnal fluctuation at low flow caused by mills and irrigation upstream from the station.
