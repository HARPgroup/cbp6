This river segment follows part of the flow of Piscataway near Tappahannock, VA. Gage 01669000 is located in Essex County, VA (Lat 37 52'37", Long 76 54'03") approximately 4.2 miles southwest of Tappahannock, VA. Drainage area is 27.9 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1951 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
