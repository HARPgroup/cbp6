This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Branch Potomac River near Springfield, WV. Gage 01608500 is located in Hampshire County, WV (Lat 39 26'49", Long 78 39'16") approximately 2.0 miles east of Springfield, WV. Drainage area is 1,461 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1894 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this are a that would affect the flow conditions.
