This river segment follows part of the flow of the South Branch of the Potomac River at Franklin, WV. Gage 01605500 is located in Pendleton County, WV (Lat 38 38'08", Long 79 20'17") approximately 0.5 miles southwest of Franklin, WV. Drainage area is 179 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1940 and took data until Sep. 1969.  It also took data between Oct. 1976 and the present.
