This river segment follows part of the flow of the Bullpasture River near Williamsville, VA. Gage 02015700 is located in Bath County, VA (Lat 38 11'43", Long 79 34'14"). Drainage area is 110 sq. miles. This gage has been taking data since 1960 continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
