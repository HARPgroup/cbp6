This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Fork of the Shenandoah River at Mount Jackson, VA. Gage 01633000 is located in Shenandoah County, VA (Lat 38 44'44", Long 78 38'21") approximately 0.4 miles downstream from Mill Creek. Drainage area is 508 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and has been taking data periodically until now.  Some diversion during low flow for irrigation at points upstream from station. Virginia Department of Emergency Services gage-height radio transmitter at station. Maximum discharge, 103,000 ft³/s, Sept. 6, 1996, from rating curve extended above 19,000 ft³/s on basis of peak runoff for stations at Cootes Store and near Strasburg.
