This river segment follows part of the flow of Dunlap Creek near Covington, VA. Gage 02013000 is located in Alleghany County, VA (Lat 37 48'10", Long 80 02'50") approximately 3.0 miles west of Covington. Drainage area is 162 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is an occasional diurnal fluctuation caused by a dam 7.9 mi upstream from station and a U.S. Army Corps of Engineers satellite precipitation and gage-height telemeter at station.
