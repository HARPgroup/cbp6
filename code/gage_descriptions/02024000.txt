This river segment follows part of the flow of the Maury River near Buena Vista, VA. Gage 02024000 is located in Rockbridge County, VA (Lat 37 45'45", Long 79 23'30") approximately 2.8 miles northwest of Buena Vista, VA. Drainage area is 647 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and is still taking data currently. Since 1966, some regulation at times by Lake Merriweather on Little Calfpasture River.
