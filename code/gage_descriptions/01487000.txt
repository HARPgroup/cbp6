This river segment follows part of the flow of the Nanticoke River near Bridgeville, DE. Gage 01487000 is located in Sussex County, VA (Lat 38 43'42", Long 75 33'42.7") approximately 2.5 miles southeast of Bridgeville, DE. Drainage area is 75.4 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
