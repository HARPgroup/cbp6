This river segment follows part of the flow of Seneca Creek near Dawsonville, MD. Gage 01645000 is located in Montgomery County, MD (Lat 39 07'41.1", Long 77 20'08.8") approximately 0.5 miles east of Dawsonville. Drainage area is 101 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1930 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at the station and a small diversion at times for irrigation upstream from the station.
