This river segment follows part of the flow of the North Branch Potomac River at Steyer, MD. Gage 01595000 is located in Garrett County, MD (Lat 39 18'06.8", Long 79 18'24.8") approximately 2.0 miles northeast of Gorman. Drainage area is 73.1 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1956 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are occasional diversions and regulation from an unknown source upstream from station.There is also a U.S. Army Corps of Engineers satellite data-collection platform at the station.
