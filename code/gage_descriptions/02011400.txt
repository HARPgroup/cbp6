This river segment follows part of the flow of the Jackson River near Bacova, VA. Gage 02011400 is located in Bath County, VA (Lat 38 02'32", Long 79 52'54") approximately 2.2 miles southwest of Bacova. Drainage area is 157 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1974 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is a U.S. Army Corps of Engineers satellite water temperature, precipitation and gage-height telemeter at station.
