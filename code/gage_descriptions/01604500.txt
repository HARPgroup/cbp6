This river segment follows part of the flow of Patterson Creek near Headsville, WV. Gage 01604500 is located in Mineral County, WV (Lat 39 26'35", Long 78 49'20") approximately 4.0 miles northeast of Headsville. Drainage area is 221 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no known anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
