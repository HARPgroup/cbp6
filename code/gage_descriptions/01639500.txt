This river segment follows part of the flow of Big Pipe Creek at Bruceville, MD. Gage 01639500 is located in Carroll County, VA (Lat 39 36'44.5", Long 77 14'14.8") approximately 500 feet downstream from Bruceville, MD. Drainage area is 102 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1947 and is still taking data currently. Occasional diversion for irrigation upstream from station. U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station.
