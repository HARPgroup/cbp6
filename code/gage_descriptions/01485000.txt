This river segment follows part of the flow of the Pocomoke River near Willards, MD. Gage 01485000 is located in Worcester County, VA (Lat 38 23'20", Long 75 19'28") approximately 1.3 miles east of Willards, VA. Drainage area is 60.5 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1949 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
