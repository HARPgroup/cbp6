This river segment follows part of the flow of the Northeast Branch Anacostia River at Riverdale, MD. Gage 01649500 is located in Prince George's County, MD (Lat 38 57'36.9", Long 76 55'33.5") approximately 1.8 miles downstream from Indian Creek. Drainage area is 72.8 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1938 and has been taking data continuously until now. There is some regulation at low flow by sand and gravel plants upstream from the station. There is also a U.S. Geological Survey satellite data-collection platform at station. There was a flood on August 23rd or 24th, 1933, that reached a stage of about 15.5 ft.
