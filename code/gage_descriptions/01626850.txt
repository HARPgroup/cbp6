This river segment follows part of the flow of the South River near Dooms, VA. Gage 01626850 is located in Waynesboro City, VA (Lat 38 05'19", Long 78 52'38") approximately 1.6 miles southwest of Dooms, VA. Drainage area is 148 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1974 and is still taking data currently. There are discharges from wastewater treatment plants upstream of station, originating from well fields. Maximum discharge, 21,100 ft³/s from rating curve extended above 8,100 ft³/s.
