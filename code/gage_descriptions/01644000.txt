This river segment follows part of the flow of Goose Creek near Leesburg, VA. Gage 01644000 is located in Loudoun County, VA (Lat 39 01'10", Long 77 34'40") approximately 6.7 miles south of Leesburg, VA. Drainage area is 332 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1909 and is still taking data currently. National Weather Service gage-height telemeter at station. Maximum discharge, 78,100 ft³/s, from rating curve extended above 11,000 ft³/s on basis of slope-area measurement of peak flow. Water-quality records for some prior periods have been collected at this location.
