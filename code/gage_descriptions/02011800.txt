This river segment follows part of the flow of the Jackson River below Gathright Dam, near Hot Springs, VA. Gage 02011800 is located in Alleghany County, VA (Lat 37 56'54", Long 79 56'58") approximately 7.3 miles southwest from Hot Springs, VA. Drainage area is 345 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1973 and is still taking data currently. Flow regulated since December 1979 by Lake Moomaw (station 02011795) 0.5 mi upstream; since October 1984 by Back Creek Lake 28.5 mi upstream, amount unknown; and since January 1985 by Little Back Creek Lake 31.6 mi upstream, amount unknown.
