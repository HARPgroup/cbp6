This river segment follows part of the flow of Deep Creek near Mannboro, VA. Gage 02041000 is located in Amelia County, VA (Lat 37 16'59", Long 77 52'12") approximately 3.4 miles northwest of Mannboro. Drainage area is 158 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1946 and has been taking data continuously until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
