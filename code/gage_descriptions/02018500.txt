This river segment follows part of the flow of Catawba Creek near Catawba, VA. Gage 02018500 is located in Botetourt County, VA (Lat 37 38'05", Long 80 00'20") approximately 8.2 miles northeast of Catawba. Drainage area is 34.3 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1943 and has been taking data continuously until now. From October 1953 to October 1976, monthly means were adjusted for pumpage by the Citadel Cement Corporation. Statistics of monthly mean data and summary statistics for water years 1944-1952 (unregulated flow) are available in previous data books, water years 1991-1998. There are also withdrawals upstream of gage.
