This river segment follows part of the flow of the Cacapon River near Great Cacapon, WV. Gage 01611500 is located in Morgan County, WV (Lat 39 34'56", Long 78 18'36") approximately 3.0 miles southwest of Great Cacapon, WV. Drainage area is 675 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1922 and has been taking data periodically until now. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this are a that would affect the flow conditions.
