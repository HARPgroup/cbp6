This river segment follows part of the flow of the Rapidan River near Ruckersville, VA. Gage 01665500 is located in Madison County, VA (Lat 38 16'50", Long 78 20'25") approximately 3.6 miles northeast of Ruckersville, VA. Drainage area is 115 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1942 and is still taking data currently. Diversion 0.4 mi upstream from station since 1973.
