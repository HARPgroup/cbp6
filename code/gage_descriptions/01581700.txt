This river segment follows part of the flow of Winters Run near Benson, MD. Gage 01581700 is located in Harford County, MD (Lat 39 31'11.8", Long 76 22'22.7") approximately 1.2 miles northeast of Benson, MD. Drainage area is 34.8 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1967 and is still taking data currently. There are no significant anthropogenic alterations in this area that would affect the flow conditions.
