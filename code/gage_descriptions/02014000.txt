This river segment follows part of the flow of Potts Creek near Covington, VA. The gage is located in Alleghany County, VA (Lat 37 43'44", Long 80 02'33") approximately 5.2 miles southwest of Covington. Drainage area is 153 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1928 and has been taking data periodically until now. There is a U.S. Army Corps of Engineers satellite gage-height telemeter at the station.
