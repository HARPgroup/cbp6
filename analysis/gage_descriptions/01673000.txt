This river segment follows part of the flow of the Pamunkey River near Hanover, VA. The gage is located in Hanover County, VA (Lat 37°46'03", Long 77°19'57") approximately 2.0 miles east of Hanover, VA. Drainage area is 1,078 sq. miles. This gage started taking data in 1941 and is still taking data currently. Some regulations have been applied since January 1972 by Lake Anna, capacity, 373,000 acre-ft, and occasional diurnal fluctuation occurs at low flow caused by a mill upstream from station.
